Fire Dragons are known for their scorching breath and fiery temperaments. Their scales shimmer with hues of red, orange, and gold, resembling molten lava. They are formidable adversaries, capable of spewing streams of flames and causing widespread destruction with their mere presence.
Ice Dragons inhabit frozen tundras and icy caverns. Their scales are as pale as snow, adorned with frosty patterns that glisten in the sunlight. They breathe blasts of freezing air and unleash blizzards upon their foes. Despite their cold exterior, they possess a graceful and elegant demeanor.
Earth Dragons are massive creatures, covered in rugged scales resembling stone and soil. They dwell deep within the earth, emerging only to protect their territories. Their immense strength allows them to reshape landscapes with ease, and they possess an innate connection to the earth's energies.
Storm Dragons are masters of the skies, with wings spanning vast distances and bodies crackling with electricity. They command the power of thunderstorms and hurricanes, wielding lightning bolts as weapons. Their scales shimmer with iridescence, reflecting the colors of the sky during tumultuous weather.
Shadow Dragons lurk in the darkness, their forms shrouded in mystery and secrecy. They possess the ability to blend seamlessly with shadows, making them nearly invisible to the naked eye. Their eyes gleam with an otherworldly glow, and they are said to possess ancient knowledge of arcane arts and forbidden magic.
